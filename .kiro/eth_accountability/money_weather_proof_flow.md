## What Constitutes a Decision in Money Weather

A Money Weather decision represents a complete risk assessment and recommendation package that could significantly influence a user's financial planning and crisis prevention strategies.

### Decision Scope and Boundaries

**Risk Assessment Determinations**

- A decision includes the complete evaluation of cashflow risk probability across multiple time horizons with specific confidence levels
- Risk threshold classification that places the user's situation into low, medium, or high risk categories with explicit reasoning
- Compound risk analysis that identifies how multiple risk factors might interact to create systemic financial vulnerability
- External factor assessment that incorporates economic, seasonal, and personal circumstances into risk probability calculations

**Intervention Recommendations**

- Warning decisions that determine whether, when, and how to alert users about developing financial risks
- Escalation decisions that evaluate whether external resources or professional intervention should be recommended
- No-action decisions that explicitly determine when user agency should be preserved over agent intervention
- Timing recommendations that specify optimal windows for user response based on risk development patterns and user capacity

**Confidence and Uncertainty Communication**

- Confidence level assignments that communicate the reliability of risk assessments and recommendations to users
- Uncertainty range specifications that help users understand the precision limitations of predictions and guidance
- Information limitation acknowledgments that identify gaps in available data that affect decision quality
- Professional consultation recommendations that identify when expert assistance would improve decision-making beyond agent capability

### Decision Context and Dependencies

**Input Data Integration**

- Historical income and expense pattern analysis that forms the foundation for risk probability calculations
- Current liquidity and obligation assessment that determines immediate vulnerability and response capacity
- Seasonal and cyclical factor recognition that adjusts risk assessments for predictable timing variations
- User-specific constraint evaluation that tailors recommendations to individual circumstances and preferences

**Temporal Decision Context**

- Market and economic condition assessment at the time of decision that affects risk probability and mitigation strategies
- Regulatory and policy environment evaluation that influences recommendation appropriateness and compliance requirements
- User life stage and circumstance consideration that affects risk tolerance and response capacity
- System learning state that reflects the agent's current capability and confidence in different types of analysis

**Cross-Agent Coordination**

- Policy agent coordination that ensures Money Weather recommendations align with insurance and protection planning
- Tax agent coordination that considers compliance timing and cash flow implications in risk assessment
- User preference integration that respects stated priorities and constraints in recommendation development
- Professional consultation coordination that ensures appropriate referral when decision complexity exceeds agent capability

## When a Decision Becomes Final

Money Weather decisions become final when the complete risk assessment and recommendation package is delivered to the user, establishing accountability for the guidance provided at that specific moment.

### Decision Completion Criteria

**Analysis Completion**

- All relevant risk factors have been evaluated according to the decision framework appropriate for the user's circumstances
- Confidence levels have been assigned based on available information quality and historical accuracy patterns
- Uncertainty ranges have been calculated to reflect the precision limitations of available data and analysis methods
- Alternative scenarios have been considered to ensure recommendation robustness across reasonable assumption variations

**Recommendation Finalization**

- Specific guidance has been formulated that addresses the user's risk situation with actionable recommendations
- Timing specifications have been established for recommended actions based on risk development patterns and user capacity
- Resource requirements have been identified to help users understand what is needed to implement recommendations effectively
- Professional consultation triggers have been evaluated to determine when expert assistance should be recommended

**User Communication Preparation**

- Recommendation content has been prepared in formats appropriate for the user's comprehension level and decision-making capacity
- Confidence and uncertainty information has been structured to support informed user decision-making without creating confusion
- Context and reasoning have been documented to enable user understanding of recommendation basis and limitations
- Follow-up and monitoring plans have been established to track recommendation effectiveness and user response

### Finalization Triggers

**Risk Threshold Events**

- Risk probability calculations cross established thresholds that trigger specific intervention protocols
- Compound risk factors reach levels that require immediate user attention or external resource activation
- Confidence levels reach points where action or inaction decisions must be made to preserve user protection
- Timing constraints require decision finalization to provide adequate user response windows

**User Context Changes**

- New information becomes available that significantly affects risk assessment or recommendation appropriateness
- User circumstances change in ways that require updated analysis and potentially different guidance
- External factors shift sufficiently to warrant reassessment of previous risk evaluations and recommendations
- System learning updates reach points where previous analysis should be reconsidered with improved capability

**Coordination Requirements**

- Other agents require Money Weather input for their own decision-making processes
- User requests for guidance create deadlines for decision completion and communication
- Regulatory or compliance timing requirements establish external deadlines for decision finalization
- Professional consultation scheduling requires decision completion to enable effective expert engagement
